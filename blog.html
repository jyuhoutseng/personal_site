Professional Manga Artist's Supplies

A cool video about the art supplies used by famous manga artist Akira Toriyama (the creator of Dragon Ball).

What I find very interesting is the tools Toriyama used when creating his works. Many of the tools stayed the same such as his pens but there were some that would change later on for obvious reason such as his paint set. It's always very interesting seeing how experienced artists work!

My Art Supplies

My personal art supplies are very limited compared to that of Mr. Toriyama's own supplies, due mostly to the differnt mediums we use. I am more of a digital artist due to the convience (and because I like to draw as a hobby) so my techniques and supplies are amateurish at best, but it's still fun to draw manga when I have free time!
